This is a list of the valid positions that characters can be placed in.

Center: Positioned directly in the center of the screen.
Right: Positioned to the right of the screen.
Off Right: Positioned offscreen, on the right side.
Center Right: Positioned to the right of the screen, closer to the center.
Far Right: Positioned to the right of the screen, closer to the edge.
Left: Positioned to the left of the screen.
Off Left: Positioned offscreen, on the left side.
Center Left: Positioned to the left of the screen, closer to the center.
Far Left: Positioned to the left of the screen, closer to the edge.
